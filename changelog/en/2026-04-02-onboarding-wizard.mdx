New players now get a step-by-step setup wizard instead of being dropped into an empty dashboard. Match syncing is also more reliable now, especially for players with a long match history.

### Setup wizard

When you sign in for the first time, a full-screen wizard walks you through everything:

1. **Choose your server** — pick your Riot region (EUW, NA, KR, etc.)
2. **Link your Riot account** — enter your Riot ID (GameName#TagLine) to connect your account
3. **Role preferences** — optionally pick your primary and secondary roles (you can skip this)
4. **Import your matches** — your 10 most recent ranked games are imported with a live progress bar, then you land on the dashboard

### Smoother match syncing

Matches now sync in smaller chunks so nothing gets lost, even if you have hundreds of games in your history. After the setup wizard finishes, any remaining matches continue syncing in the background while you explore the dashboard.
