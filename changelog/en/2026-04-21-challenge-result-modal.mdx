title: "Challenge result popups"
tags: ["feature"]
---

When a challenge succeeds or fails after syncing matches, you now get a popup modal with the result. Completed challenges get a **confetti celebration**, while failed ones show an encouraging message with a **"Try again"** button that takes you straight to creating a new challenge.

If multiple challenges resolve in the same sync, the modal paginates through all results — successes first, then failures.
