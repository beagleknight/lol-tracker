title: "Smarter off-role handling"
tags: ["improvement"]
---

Off-role games now stand out more clearly and no longer clutter your review queue. The app treats any game outside your primary role as "off-role" — no more fuzzy secondary role distinction.

- **Stronger visual treatment**: Off-role matches are fully desaturated with a subtle elevated background so your main-role games stand out at a glance.
- **Review queue filters them out**: Off-role games no longer appear in the post-game review tab. Your review count only reflects games worth reviewing.
- **Match detail shows your position**: When you open a match, the header now shows which role you played and an "off-role" badge when it's not your main role. The review prompt is hidden for off-role games.
