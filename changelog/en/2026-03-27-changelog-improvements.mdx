The changelog page got a big upgrade.

- **Calendar-based versions**: Version numbers now follow a calendar scheme (like 2026.03.9) instead of the old format. Easier to tell when something was released.
- **Tag badges**: Each entry is labeled as Feature, Fix, or Improvement with a colored badge.
- **Filter by tag**: Click a tag in the filter bar to show only that type of entry.
- **Pagination**: Entries are split across pages (5 per page) with Previous/Next buttons, so the page stays fast as the changelog grows.
- **Translation fixes**: Fixed missing accent marks in the Spanish translations and cleaned up title casing.
