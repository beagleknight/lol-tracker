All UI text has been converted from Title Case to **sentence case** — only the first word and proper nouns (LoL Tracker, Riot, Discord, Ascent) or acronyms (KDA, CS, LP, CSV, VOD, AI, UX) remain capitalized.

This affects:

- Sidebar navigation labels
- Dashboard card headings and stats
- Match list filters and column headers
- Review and coaching flow labels
- Settings page sections
- Changelog entry titles (both EN and ES)

Sentence case gives the app a more natural, polished feel and moves away from the "AI-generated" look of pervasive Title Case.
