tags: ["improvement"]
---

All text in the app now uses **sentence case**. Only the first word, proper nouns (LoL Tracker, Riot, Discord, Ascent), and acronyms (KDA, CS, LP, CSV, VOD, AI, UX) stay capitalized.

This covers:

- Sidebar navigation labels
- Dashboard card headings and stats
- Match list filters and column headers
- Review and coaching flow labels
- Settings page sections
- Changelog entry titles (both EN and ES)

The app reads more naturally now, without the stiff Title Case that made everything feel auto-generated.
