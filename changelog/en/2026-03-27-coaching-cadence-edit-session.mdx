tags: ["feature"]

- **Coaching cadence on the dashboard**: A new card shows how many days since your last completed coaching session. It turns green when you're on track (under 14 days), yellow when you're due soon (14 to 21 days), and red when you're overdue (over 21 days). Click it to go straight to the session.

- **Edit coaching sessions**: You can now edit any session from its detail page. Change the coach name, date/time, VOD match, or topics. For completed sessions, you can also update duration and notes. Just click the pencil icon in the header.
