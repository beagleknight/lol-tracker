Two small quality-of-life improvements to help you find things faster.

### Time filter is now front and center

The time filter (seasons, date ranges) used to be hidden at the very bottom of the sidebar. It's now right below the logo so you always know which period you're looking at.

### Active and completed action items are visually separated

When viewing all action items, active tasks now appear first with a clear "Completed" divider before finished ones. No more scanning each row to figure out which are done.
