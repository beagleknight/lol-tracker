You can now choose how often you want coaching sessions — weekly, biweekly, every three weeks, or monthly. The dashboard coaching widget adapts its urgency indicators to your chosen cadence instead of using fixed thresholds.

- **Configurable coaching cadence**: Pick your preferred coaching frequency from preset buttons in the new Preferences tab. The dashboard uses your setting to determine when sessions are "on track", "due soon", or "overdue".
- **Settings redesign with tabs**: The settings page is now organized into three tabs — Account (Riot account, language, region, roles), Preferences (coaching cadence), and Duo (duo partner management).
- **Smarter dashboard widget**: When you're on track, the widget shows how many days until your next session is due. The buffer window is always 7 days past your cadence before marking overdue.
