You can now filter your match data by League of Legends season or a custom date range. A new filter in the sidebar lets you scope the dashboard, matches list, and analytics to a specific time period.

### Filter by season

Pick a full year, a specific season (like Demacia or Pandemonium), or set a custom date range. The filter applies globally — dashboard stats, recent games, and analytics all update to show only matches from that period.

### Custom date range

Need a specific window? Choose "Custom range" and pick start and end dates with the date picker. Your filter persists across page navigations.
