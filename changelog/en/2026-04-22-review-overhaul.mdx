The way you review games and track action items has been completely reworked to be faster and more focused.

### Simpler game reviews

Reviewing a game used to be a two-step process — post-game notes first, then a separate VOD review later. Now it's a **single pass**: open a game, click the topics that mattered (good or bad), write any notes you want, and save. Done.

The review page has just two tabs: **Pending** and **Reviewed**. No more juggling between three tabs.

### Everything in one place on match detail

When you look at a reviewed game, all your notes are now in a single card right at the top — highlight topics, lowlight topics, and your written notes together. No more scrolling to the bottom to find what you wrote.

<div className="changelog-image-grid">

![Before: notes and VOD review split into separate cards at the bottom](/changelog/review-action-items-overhaul/before-match-detail.png)

![After: unified review card above the scoreboard](/changelog/review-action-items-overhaul/after-match-detail-annotated.png)

</div>

### Action items track outcomes per game

Action items now have two statuses: **active** or **completed**. But the bigger change is per-game outcomes. Each game you play, you can mark how you did on each action item — nailed it, forgot, or unsure. Over time you'll see the pattern: how often you actually follow through.
