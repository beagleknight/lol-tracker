You can now link **up to 5 Riot accounts** to track stats on all your accounts — main, smurfs, region alts, whatever you play on.

- **Link multiple accounts**: Head to the new **Accounts** tab in Settings to add more Riot accounts. Each one gets its own match history, rank tracking, goals, and analytics — nothing is mixed together.
- **Switch between accounts**: Use the account switcher at the bottom of the sidebar to flip between accounts. All your data (matches, rank, goals, highlights) updates to show the selected account.
- **Main account protection**: Your first linked account is marked as "main" by default. You can change which account is main, but the main account can never be removed — so you won't accidentally lose your primary tracking.
- **Custom labels**: Give each account a nickname (like "Smurf", "EUW Alt", "Flex only") so you can tell them apart at a glance.
- **Coaching stays unified**: Your coaching sessions, action items, and matchup notes aren't tied to a specific account — they follow you across all your accounts.
