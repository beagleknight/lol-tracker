title: "Goal setting with progress tracking"
tags: ["feature"]
---

Set rank-based goals and track your progress over time.

- **Set a rank target**: Choose your target tier and division (e.g. "Reach Platinum IV") with an optional soft deadline.
- **Progress tracking**: Your progress is calculated automatically based on rank snapshots and shown as a percentage bar.
- **Dashboard widget**: See your active goal and progress at a glance on the dashboard.
- **Auto-achieve**: Goals are automatically marked as achieved when your rank meets or exceeds the target after a sync.
- **Retire goals**: Changed your mind? Retire a goal and start a new one. Only one active goal at a time.
- **Goals page**: View your active goal, past goals (achieved and retired), and manage your history.
