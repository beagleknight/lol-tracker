title: "CS per minute on match cards and dashboard"
tags: ["improvement"]
---

Match cards now show **CS per minute** alongside total CS (e.g. "240 CS (8.0/min)") so you can compare farming efficiency across games of different lengths.

The dashboard's "Avg KDA" card also shows your **average CSPM** from your last 10 games instead of raw CS totals.
