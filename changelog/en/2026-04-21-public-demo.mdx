You can now **try the app without signing up** via the new public demo at `/demo`.

- **"Try the demo" button** on the landing page takes you straight in.
- **All 8 pages fully functional**: dashboard, analytics, matches, match detail, review, coaching, scout, and challenges — all populated with realistic sample data.
- **Read-only mode**: write actions (scheduling sessions, creating challenges, editing reviews, etc.) are hidden so the demo stays clean.
- **Locked sections**: settings, duo, and feedback are shown grayed out with a sign-up prompt.
- **Gold banner** at the top reminds you it's a demo and links to sign up.
