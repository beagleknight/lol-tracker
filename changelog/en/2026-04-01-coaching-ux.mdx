The coaching workflow got a bunch of fixes to make scheduling, completing sessions, and managing action items smoother.

### VOD selector pagination

When picking a match for your session, you now see 10 games at a time instead of all 50 in one long scroll. Hit "Show more" to load the next batch.

![VOD selector showing all matches in a flat scroll](/changelog/coaching-ux/before-new-session-top.png)

![VOD selector showing 10 matches with Show more button](/changelog/coaching-ux/after-vod-annotated.png)

</div>

### Complete sessions from the hub

Overdue sessions now show a "Complete" button right on the coaching hub card. Sessions that are 2+ days past their date get a red border so they stand out.

<div className="changelog-image-grid">

![Coaching hub with no completion option](/changelog/coaching-ux/before-coaching-hub.png)

![Coaching hub with overdue indicator and Complete button](/changelog/coaching-ux/after-hub-annotated.png)

</div>

### Color-coded action item badges

Action item badges now use color to show status: green for completed, gold for in progress, and neutral for pending. Works everywhere action items appear.

<div className="changelog-image-grid">

![Action items with identical badges](/changelog/coaching-ux/before-action-items-page.png)

![Action items with color-coded status badges](/changelog/coaching-ux/after-action-items-annotated.png)

</div>

### Standalone action items

You can now create action items on their own, without tying them to a coaching session. Use the "+ Add" button on the action items page.

### Focus areas vs. topics covered

When scheduling a session, your planned focus areas are now kept separate from the topics you actually covered. After completing a session, you can see both side by side.
