A brand new **Review page** brings all your post-game analysis into one place:

- **Post-Game tab**: add highlights, lowlights, and quick notes right after playing
- **VOD Review tab**: revisit games you've already noted — add deeper VOD analysis
- **Completed tab**: browse fully reviewed games with inline editing

The sidebar now shows a **gold badge** with the number of games waiting for review, so you always know where you left off.

### Visual improvements

- Better color contrast across the app — easier on the eyes in dark mode
- Improved highlight and lowlight tag styling on the review cards
- Match detail page now shows highlights right below stats, not buried at the bottom
