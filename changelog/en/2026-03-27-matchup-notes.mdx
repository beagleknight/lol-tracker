title: "Matchup-specific notes"
tags: ["feature"]
---

Keep persistent notes about champion matchups, available right when you need them.

- **Per-matchup notes**: Write notes for any enemy champion on the scout page. They stick around across games, ready for your next encounter.
- **Champion-specific and general**: Add notes tied to a specific champion pairing (e.g. "Yone vs Syndra") or general notes that apply no matter who you're playing.
- **Inline editing**: Click any note to edit, save, or delete it. Changes save instantly.
- **Visible in match detail**: Your matchup notes show up on match detail pages too, with a link back to the scout page if you want to edit them.
