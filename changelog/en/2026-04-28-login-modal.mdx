title: "Sign in now opens as a popup over the home page"
tags: ["improvement"]
---

Clicking **Sign in** or **Get started** on the home page now opens the login form as a popup right there — no more jumping to a separate page. You stay on the home page the whole time.

Also, if you sign out, you now land on the home page instead of getting stuck on a blank login screen. And if you do end up on the login page directly (from a bookmark or link), there's a **Back to home** link in the top left.
