The changelog page got a top-to-bottom refresh with better image viewing and cleaner writing.

### Image zoom

- **Smooth transitions**: images fade in smoothly instead of popping into view.
- **Easy to close**: a visible X button in the top corner, plus click outside or press Escape to dismiss.
- **Clear framing**: zoomed images have a subtle shadow so they stand out from the background.

### Better image layout

- **Smaller thumbnails**: images are smaller by default so they don't dominate the text.
- **Zoom hint**: a magnifying glass icon appears on hover, so you know images are clickable.
- **Captions**: every image now shows a caption below it.
- **Side-by-side grids**: before/after image pairs sit next to each other instead of stacking vertically.

### Cleaner writing

All changelog entries rewritten in plain, casual language. No more technical jargon or implementation details.
