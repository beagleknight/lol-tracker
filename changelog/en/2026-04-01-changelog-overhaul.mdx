The changelog page got a top-to-bottom refresh, and the image lightbox now works properly.

### Lightbox

- **Fade transition**: images now fade in smoothly instead of popping into view.
- **Close button**: a visible X button in the top corner, plus click-outside and Escape to dismiss.
- **Shadow frame**: zoomed images have a subtle shadow and border so they stand out from the overlay.

### Images

- **Smaller thumbnails**: images are smaller by default so they don't dominate the text.
- **Zoom affordance**: a magnifying glass icon appears on hover, so you know images are clickable.
- **Captions**: every image now shows a caption below it, pulled from the alt text.
- **2-column grids**: before/after image pairs sit side by side instead of stacking vertically.

### Copy

- All 22 changelog entries (EN and ES) rewritten in plain, casual language.
- Removed all em-dashes from the changelog and i18n files.
- No more engineering jargon, feature IDs, or implementation details.
