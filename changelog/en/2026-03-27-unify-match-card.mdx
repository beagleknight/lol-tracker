tags: ["improvement"]

Recent games on the dashboard now look and work the same as the ones on the matches page, just in a more compact layout.

- **Highlights on the dashboard**: Your post-game highlights and lowlights are now visible in the recent games list, not just on the matches page.
- **Review status icons**: You can quickly see which recent games have been reviewed and which still need attention.
- **Consistent result indicator**: Every match row across the app now uses the same thin vertical bar to show win/loss.
- **Rune and scout links**: The compact cards still show your rune keystone and link to the scout page.
