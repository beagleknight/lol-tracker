tags: ["improvement"]

Both dark and light themes got a readability pass with better contrast and clearer visual layers.

- **Dark mode**: Background surfaces are slightly lighter now, and the sidebar stays darker than the content area so you can tell them apart at a glance.
- **Light mode**: Switched to a white-card-on-grey-background look that feels cleaner and less flat.
- **Sharper corners**: Cards and panels use tighter border radius for a more precise, tool-like feel.
- **Contrast compliance**: Every text/background combination meets WCAG AA contrast ratios.
