Goals have evolved into **challenges** — a more flexible system to help you improve at the game.

You can still set rank targets with a deadline (like the old goals), but now you can also create **over-games challenges** that track a specific stat across a number of matches. For example: keep your CS/min above 7 for 10 games, or stay under 5 deaths for 15 games. Each challenge shows your progress and success rate as you play.

You can also link challenges to **topics** (like laning phase, vision control, or teamfighting) to stay focused on what you're working on.
