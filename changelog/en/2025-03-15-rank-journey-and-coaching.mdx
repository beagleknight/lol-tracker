### Rank Journey

The analytics page now features a **Rank Journey** chart that tracks your LP over time:

- See your **peak rank** highlighted on the chart
- **Milestone markers** for the first time you reach a new tier
- Framed around growth — promotions say "Reached Gold I" instead of dry stat changes

### Coaching sessions

A full coaching workflow to help you improve with structure:

- **Schedule sessions** with a coach name, date, focus areas, and an optional VOD
- **Link matches** to sessions so you can review them in context
- **Action items** with status tracking (pending, in progress, completed)
- A dedicated **Action Items page** to filter and manage tasks across all sessions
