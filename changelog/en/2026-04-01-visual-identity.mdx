The whole app got a fresh look. New logo, rank-themed colors, better typography, and small animations that make everything feel more polished.

### Custom logo

The old generic swords icon is gone. LoL Tracker now has its own diamond/shield logo with a crosshair detail, visible in the sidebar and login page.

</div>

### Dashboard rank card

Your rank card now shows the official rank emblem with a gold-accented border and your rank name front and center.

![Dashboard with plain rank card](/changelog/visual-identity/before-dashboard.png)

</div>

### Match card win/loss tints

Match cards now have subtle green (win) or red (loss) background tints so you can scan results at a glance. They also lift slightly on hover.

<div className="changelog-image-grid">

![Match list with identical card backgrounds](/changelog/visual-identity/before-matches.png)

![Match list with tinted win/loss backgrounds](/changelog/visual-identity/after-matches-annotated.png)

</div>

### Monospace stats

All numbers (KDA, CS, gold, vision, duration, LP, win rates) now use the same monospace font everywhere. No more misaligned digits.

### Shared empty states

Pages that have no data yet (scout, matches, analytics, review, coaching, goals, action items) now show the same clean empty state instead of random one-offs.

![Empty state on the scout page](/changelog/visual-identity/after-scout-annotated.png)

### Entrance animations

Page headers and sections slide in with a subtle stagger when you load a page. If your OS is set to reduce motion, these are automatically turned off.
