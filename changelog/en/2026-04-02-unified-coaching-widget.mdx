The two separate coaching cards on the dashboard have been merged into one. Everything you need — your next session, how long since your last one, and whether you're keeping up your coaching rhythm — is now in a single card.

- **One card, all the info**: Next session details (coach, date, VOD status) and your coaching cadence are shown together instead of in separate cards.
- **Cadence always visible**: Even when you have a session scheduled, the card shows how long it's been since your last one with a color-coded status badge (on track, due soon, or overdue).
- **Schedule next**: When you don't have an upcoming session, a "Schedule next" button appears right in the card.
