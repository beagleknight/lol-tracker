A round of fixes to make the dashboard more accurate and easier to read.

- **Accurate win rate**: The session win rate now reflects exactly 10 competitive games, with remakes properly excluded.
- **Stat cards don't look clickable anymore**: The rank, win rate, streak, and KDA cards no longer have a hover effect since they're just informational.
- **Goal progress shows your target**: The goal widget now displays both your current rank and target rank, with tier milestones on the progress bar.
- **Action item status labels**: Action items on the dashboard now show "In progress" or "Pending" badges next to the colored dot.
- **Overdue coaching warning**: Sessions that are 2+ days past-due show a red "Ready to complete" badge instead of the default gold one.
- **LP trend with context**: The rank card now says "+X LP in last N days" instead of the vague "+X LP recently."
