Several usability improvements to the dashboard based on the UX audit findings:

- **Accurate win rate stats** — The session win rate now always reflects exactly 10 competitive games, excluding remakes from the query instead of fetching them and filtering client-side.
- **Stat cards no longer look clickable** — Removed the hover-lift effect from the four informational stat cards (Rank, Win Rate, Streak, KDA) since they are read-only.
- **Goal progress shows target rank** — The goal widget now displays both your current rank and target rank, with tier milestone markers on the progress bar.
- **Action item status labels** — Dashboard action items now show "In Progress" or "Pending" badges alongside the colored dot for clearer status at a glance.
- **Overdue coaching session warning** — Sessions past-due by 2+ days now show a red "Ready to complete · X days ago" badge instead of the neutral gold one.
- **LP trend with time context** — The rank card now shows "+X LP in last N days" instead of the vague "+X LP recently".
