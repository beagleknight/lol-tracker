The app now supports all Riot server regions, so players outside Europe can link their accounts and sync matches. This is the first step toward multi-user beta readiness.

### What changed

- **Server region selector in settings** — choose your Riot server (EUW, NA, KR, etc.) from a dropdown in the Riot account card. The region is saved and used for all API calls.
- **All Riot API calls are region-aware** — match history, rank data, and account lookups now route to the correct regional and platform endpoints based on your selected region.
- **Onboarding redirect** — new users who haven't completed setup are automatically redirected to an onboarding page instead of landing on an empty dashboard.
- **NewPlayer demo account** — a third demo user with no Riot account linked, for testing the onboarding flow.
