You can now select your Riot server region so the app syncs matches from the correct server. Previously the app only worked with EUW — now players on NA, KR, LAN, and all other regions can use the tracker properly.

### What changed

- **Region selector in settings** — choose your server (EUW, NA, KR, etc.) from the Riot account card in Settings. Your selection is saved and used for all match syncs and rank lookups.
- **Region reminder** — if you have a linked Riot account but haven't selected a region yet, a banner on the dashboard, matches, and review pages will remind you to set it.
