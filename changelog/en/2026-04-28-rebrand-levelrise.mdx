We've rebranded from LoL Tracker to **LevelRise** with a new logo, new colors, and a fresh identity. The app still does everything it did before — tracking your matches, coaching sessions, and helping you climb — but now with a look that's distinctly ours.

### What changed

- **New name** — "LevelRise" with the tagline _Clarity for your climb._
- **New logo** — ascending bars with an upward arrow, in a gold-to-teal gradient
- **Updated colors** — deeper darks, warmer golds, and a refined palette across the whole app
- **New font** — Plus Jakarta Sans replaces Inter for a more modern feel
- **New favicon** — the logo mark now shows up in your browser tab
