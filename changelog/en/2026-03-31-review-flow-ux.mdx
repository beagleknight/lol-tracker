The review page is easier to work through now, with better card layout, smarter sorting, and a few small tweaks that save you time.

### Before & after

<div className="changelog-image-grid">

![Review page before changes](/changelog/review-flow-ux/before-review.png)

![Review cards collapsed with priority sorting and Suggested badge](/changelog/review-flow-ux/after-review.png)

</div>

- **Collapsed cards**: Review cards start collapsed so you see all your pending games at once. Click to expand one at a time. Finishing a review auto-opens the next card.
- **Priority sorting**: Games that are more worth reviewing (losses, high deaths, close games) float to the top. The most instructive match gets a "Suggested" badge.

- **"Mark all reviewed" moved**: The bulk-skip action is now behind a "..." menu so you don't accidentally skip everything.

![Overflow menu with mark all reviewed option](/changelog/review-flow-ux/after-overflow-menu.png)

- **Disabled button tooltips**: When Save or Skip are greyed out, hovering them explains why (e.g. no content entered yet).

![Tooltip on a disabled Save button explaining why it is inactive](/changelog/review-flow-ux/after-disabled-tooltip.png)

- **Generic VOD labels**: The VOD link field no longer says "Ascent VOD Link." It just says "VOD link" and works with any provider.
- **Session progress**: A small counter shows how many games you've reviewed so far this session.

![Session progress counter showing reviewed games out of total](/changelog/review-flow-ux/after-session-progress.png)
