Streamlined the post-game and VOD review workflow with several quality-of-life improvements:

### Before

![Review page before changes](/changelog/review-flow-ux/before-review.png)

### After

- **Collapsed cards** — review cards now start collapsed with only the match header visible, accordion-style. Click to expand one at a time, and completing a review auto-advances to the next card.
- **Priority sorting** — games are sorted by review value (losses, high deaths, close games float to the top) with a "Suggested" badge on the most instructive matches.

![Collapsed cards with priority sorting and Suggested badge](/changelog/review-flow-ux/after-review.png)

- **Mark All Reviewed relocated** — moved the bulk-skip action behind a "..." overflow menu to prevent accidental clicks.

![Overflow menu with Mark All Reviewed](/changelog/review-flow-ux/after-overflow-menu.png)

- **Disabled button tooltips** — Save and Skip buttons now show a tooltip explaining why they're disabled when no content has been entered.

![Tooltip on disabled Save button](/changelog/review-flow-ux/after-disabled-tooltip.png)

- **Generic VOD labels** — "Ascent VOD Link" renamed to "VOD Link" with a generic placeholder, making the field provider-agnostic.
- **Session progress** — a "X of Y reviewed this session" indicator tracks your progress while reviewing.

![Session progress indicator](/changelog/review-flow-ux/after-session-progress.png)
