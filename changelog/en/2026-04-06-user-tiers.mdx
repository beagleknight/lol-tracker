The app now distinguishes between **free** and **premium** accounts. Premium-only features are clearly marked so you always know what's available to you.

- **Duo analytics are premium-only**: Free users see a "Premium feature" message instead of the duo partner page. The sidebar shows a lock icon on the Duo link so you know before clicking.
- **AI insights are premium-only**: Free users can still open the AI insight panel, but generating insights requires a premium account.
- **Extra accounts are premium-only**: Free users can link their main Riot account. Linking additional accounts (smurfs, alts) requires premium.
- **Settings duo tab is premium-only**: Free users see the premium gate when opening the duo tab in settings, consistent with the duo analytics page.
- **Premium indicator in sidebar**: Premium users see a gold crown icon next to their name so they always know their account status.
