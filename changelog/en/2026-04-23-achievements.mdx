Track your milestones and unlock badges as you play. 30 achievements across 7 categories — from reviewing matches to pulling off a flawless game — with a tiered rank system that mirrors League's own.

### Achievement badges and tiers

Each achievement has a custom badge with category-specific colors. Count-based achievements progress through League-inspired tiers (Iron, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond), shown as colored rings around the badge.

### Unlock notifications

When you earn achievements, a paginated modal celebrates each unlock. If you already have history in the app, your existing data is evaluated automatically — you'll see a special "We checked your history..." screen.

![Achievement unlock modal showing backfill notification with paginated navigation](/changelog/achievements/after-unlock-modal.png)

### Secret achievements

Some achievements are hidden until you unlock them. You'll see "???" placeholder cards for 7 secret achievements — mostly funny combat moments like dying 15+ times or winning with 0 deaths.
