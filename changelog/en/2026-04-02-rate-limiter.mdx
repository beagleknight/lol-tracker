Match syncing is now much smarter when multiple players use the app at the same time.

### What changed

- **No more stepping on each other**: When several players sync at once, the app now takes turns so nobody's sync gets interrupted or slowed down by others.
- **Automatic speed control**: The app reads how much of its request allowance it has left and slows down or speeds up accordingly. No more sudden timeouts or failed syncs.
- **One sync at a time per player**: If you have multiple tabs open and accidentally trigger sync twice, the duplicate is ignored instead of causing errors.
- **Crash-safe**: If a sync gets interrupted (e.g., you close the tab), the lock automatically expires after 5 minutes so your next sync won't be blocked.

### What you'll notice

- If someone else is currently syncing, you'll see **"Waiting for your turn..."** instead of an error. Your sync will start automatically once a slot opens up.
- Sync speed may vary — the app is deliberately pacing itself to stay within its request limits. This is normal and expected.
