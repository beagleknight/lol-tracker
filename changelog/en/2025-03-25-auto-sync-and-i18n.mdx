Your matches now **sync automatically**. No more clicking the sync button every time you play.

- **On login**: Your latest matches are fetched as soon as you sign in.
- **When you come back**: Switching back to the LoL Tracker tab triggers a sync if it's been a while.
- **In the background**: A check runs every ~15 minutes to catch new games.

You'll only see a notification when **new matches are actually found**, so no more empty toast messages.

### Spanish support

The app is now available in **Spanish**. Head to settings to switch your language. Date and number formats are also configurable independently.
