Your matches now **sync automatically** — no more clicking the sync button every time you play.

- **On login**: your latest matches are fetched as soon as you sign in
- **When you come back**: switching back to the LoL Tracker tab triggers a sync if it's been a while
- **In the background**: a periodic check runs every ~15 minutes to catch new games

You'll only see a notification when **new matches are actually found** — no more noisy toasts for empty syncs.

### Spanish support

The app is now available in **Spanish**! Head to **Settings** to switch your language. Date and number formats are also configurable independently — pick the locale that feels right for you.
