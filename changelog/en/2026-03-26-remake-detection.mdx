title: "Remake detection"
tags: ["fix"]
---

Games that ended as **remakes** (early surrenders before 5 minutes) are now properly detected and excluded from your stats.

- **Automatic detection**: Remakes are flagged during sync and show a grey "R" badge instead of win/loss.
- **Excluded from all stats**: Win rates, streaks, matchup analytics, duo stats, and coaching progress no longer count remakes.
- **Filterable**: Use the "Remakes" option in the result filter on the matches page to find them.
- **Retroactive fix**: Existing matches have been updated to correctly flag old remakes too.
