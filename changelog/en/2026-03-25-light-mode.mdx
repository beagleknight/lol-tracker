tags: ["feature"]

The app now supports **light mode** alongside the original dark theme.

- **Theme toggle**: Use the button in the sidebar to switch between dark, light, and system (follows your OS preference).
- **Charts adapt**: All charts and analytics adjust their colors to the active theme, so nothing looks washed out.
- **Everywhere**: Wins, losses, streaks, coaching markers, changelog glows, gradients, and more all respect the current theme.
