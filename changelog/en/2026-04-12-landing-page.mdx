When someone visits LevelRise for the first time, they now see a proper welcome page instead of being thrown straight to a login screen with no context.

The landing page explains what the app does — match tracking, post-game review, analytics, and coaching — with screenshots of each feature. There's a sign-in button at the top and bottom, and a language toggle so visitors can read everything in English or Spanish.

If you're already signed in, nothing changes — you'll go straight to your dashboard as usual.
