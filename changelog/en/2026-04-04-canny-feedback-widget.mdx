There's now a **Feedback** button in the sidebar. Click it to open a slide-out panel where you can submit feature requests, report bugs, or vote on ideas from other players — all without leaving the app.

- **Sidebar feedback button**: A new "Feedback" item sits in the sidebar between Settings and Admin. One click opens the panel; press Escape or click outside to close it.
- **Integrated feedback board**: The panel shows the full feedback board where you can post suggestions, upvote existing ones, and track their status.
- **Automatically linked to your account**: Your feedback is tied to your account, so you'll get notified when something you requested ships.
