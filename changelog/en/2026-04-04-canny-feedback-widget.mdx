There's now a feedback button in the bottom-right corner of every page. Click it to open a panel where you can submit feature requests, report bugs, or vote on ideas from other players — all without leaving the app.

- **Feedback button**: A gold floating button appears on every page. Click it to open the feedback panel, click again or press Escape to close it.
- **Integrated feedback board**: The panel shows the full feedback board where you can post suggestions, upvote existing ones, and track their status.
- **Automatically linked to your account**: Your feedback is tied to your account, so you'll get notified when something you requested ships.
