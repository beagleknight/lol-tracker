There's now a **Feedback** page in the app. Click "Feedback" in the sidebar to submit feature requests, report bugs, or vote on ideas from other players.

- **Dedicated feedback page**: A new "Feedback" link in the sidebar takes you to a full page where you can browse and post suggestions, upvote existing ones, and track their status.
- **Automatically linked to your account**: Your feedback is tied to your account, so you'll get notified when something you requested ships.
