Every match now shows which lane you played, and you can set your preferred roles in settings so the app knows what's on-role and what's autofill.

### How it works

- **Position icon on every match card** across matches, dashboard, review, and scout pages.
- **Color-coded by relevance**: gold for your main role, muted for secondary, orange for off-role/autofill.
- **Off-role games get a dashed border** so your main-role games stand out visually.
- **Role preferences in settings**: pick a primary and secondary role. The app uses these to decide what's on-role.

Position data is detected automatically from your matches.
