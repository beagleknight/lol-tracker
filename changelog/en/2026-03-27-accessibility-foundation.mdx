The app now meets **WCAG 2.1 AA** accessibility standards, with automated checks to keep it that way.

- **Screen reader support**: All icon-only buttons now have descriptive labels, progress bars are properly announced, and interactive elements use correct roles.
- **Color contrast**: Text and backgrounds across the app now meet the 4.5:1 contrast ratio requirement.
- **Keyboard navigation**: A "Skip to main content" link, visible focus indicators on all interactive elements, and keyboard-operable badges.
- **Reduced motion**: Animations and hover effects are automatically turned off when your OS is set to reduce motion.
- **Automated scanning**: Every page is scanned for accessibility violations as part of the test suite, so regressions get caught early.
