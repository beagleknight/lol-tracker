tags: ["improvement"]
---

The app is now easier to use for everyone, with better support for screen readers, keyboards, and different visual needs.

- **Screen reader support**: all icon-only buttons now have descriptive labels, progress bars are properly announced, and all interactive elements are correctly identified.
- **Readable colors**: text and backgrounds across the app have enough contrast to be easy to read in both themes.
- **Keyboard navigation**: a "Skip to main content" shortcut, visible focus indicators on all interactive elements, and everything is usable without a mouse.
- **Reduced motion**: animations and hover effects are automatically turned off when your OS is set to reduce motion.
