The app now meets **WCAG 2.1 AA** accessibility standards with automated tooling to keep it that way.

- **Screen reader support** — all icon-only buttons now have descriptive labels, progress bars are properly announced, and interactive elements use correct ARIA roles
- **Color contrast** — sidebar headings, win/loss pills, and destructive buttons now meet the 4.5:1 contrast ratio requirement
- **Keyboard navigation** — added a "Skip to main content" link, visible focus indicators on all interactive elements, and topic/focus-area badges are now keyboard-operable
- **Reduced motion** — animations, transitions, and hover effects are automatically disabled when your OS is set to reduce motion
- **Automated scanning** — new axe-core Playwright tests scan every page for violations, and ESLint jsx-a11y rules catch issues at compile time
