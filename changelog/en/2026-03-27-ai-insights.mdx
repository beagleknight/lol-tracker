Get personalized coaching advice powered by AI, right where you need it.

- **Matchup insights**: On the scout page, generate AI advice based on your matchup history, rune performance, and personal notes.
- **Post-game analysis**: On any match detail page, get an AI review of your performance with tips based on your stats, highlights, and goals.
- **Cached results**: Insights are saved so you only generate them once per matchup or match. Hit regenerate anytime if you want a fresh take.
- **Daily limit**: 10 AI generations per day. Cached results don't count against the limit.
